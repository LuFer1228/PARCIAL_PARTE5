LAS ROCAS

Las Rocas is a restaurant known for its beautiful settings and diverse culinary offerings. Located in multiple destinations, it serves a mix of Mexican and international cuisine. For instance, Las Rocas Bar & Grill in Castaic, California, is a cozy spot offering classic Mexican dishes in a vibrant, welcoming atmosphere​( Another branch, located at the Las Rocas Resort & Spa in Rosarito, Mexico, provides a luxurious dining experience with oceanfront views, serving seafood specialties like Puerto Nuevo-style lobster​( Both locations offer a great ambiance, whether you are looking for casual dining or a more elegant meal by the sea.

Menu Description: Specializes in Mexican cuisine with a focus on seafood, including local favorites like lobster and shrimp, complemented by stunning oceanfront views.

RODIZZIO

Location: Typically found in Brazilian steakhouse chains worldwide. Menu Description: Brazilian-style barbecue, known for endless servings of grilled meats like beef, chicken, and pork, served directly from skewers at your table, along with a variety of salads and sides.

GIROLATA

Location: Often found in coastal Mediterranean towns. Menu Description: Specializes in Mediterranean cuisine, offering a variety of fresh seafood, pasta dishes, and grilled meats, inspired by the flavors of the Mediterranean coastline.

CREPES AND WAFFLES

Location: Multiple locations across Latin America and Spain. Menu Description: Known for its wide range of crepes, both sweet and savory, along with salads, soups, and ice cream. The restaurant focuses on fresh ingredients and creative combinations.
